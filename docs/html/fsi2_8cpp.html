Detailed Description

This is the benchmark test of fluid-structure interaction.

We consider a flow-induced vibration of an elastic beam behind a cylinder in 2D. The case can be found in Chi Zhang, Massoud Rezavand, Xiangyu Hu, Dual-criteria time stepping for weakly compressible smoothed particle hydrodynamics. Journal of Computation Physics 404 (2020) 109135.

Author
Xiangyu Hu, Chi Zhang and Luhui Han
